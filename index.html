About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of monsters in a constantly evolving world. Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating hordes of enemies and collecting power-ups to enhance your abilities.

As you progress through the game, you'll unlock new characters, weapons, and special abilities that will help you survive the increasingly difficult challenges ahead.

Game Features

Endless Gameplay

Face wave after wave of unique monsters with increasing difficulty. How long can you survive?

Character Progression

Level up your character and unlock powerful abilities and upgrades as you play.

Diverse Enemies

Battle against a variety of monsters, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns.

Power-ups & Items

Collect various power-ups and items to enhance your character's abilities and survival chances.

How to Play

Monster Survivors features intuitive controls that are easy to learn but challenging to master:

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Your character attacks automatically when enemies are in range
  • Collect experience orbs from defeated enemies to level up
  • Choose new abilities or upgrades when you level up
  • Survive as long as possible and aim for a high score!
